The onboarding wizard helps you set up your profile so Estudio Three can generate optimized daily routines tailored to your academic, athletic, and personal needs.

## Why Complete Onboarding?

The information you provide during onboarding enables the intelligent routine engine to:

- **Generate smart schedules** - Automatically allocate study time based on subject difficulty
- **Balance cognitive and physical load** - Optimize your daily energy expenditure
- **Respect your commitments** - Work around classes, training, and events
- **Personalize your experience** - Adapt to your focus preferences and attention span

<Tip>
The more accurate your information, the better your routines will be. You can always update your profile later in Settings.
</Tip>

## The 4-Step Wizard

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step 1: Personal Information">
    Provide your basic details to get started.
    
    **Required fields:**
    - **Name** - Must be at least 3 characters
    - **Age** - Must be 8 years or older
    
    The system validates your inputs in real-time and shows helpful error messages if needed.
    
    <Note>
    Your name and age help the system adjust cognitive load expectations and communication style.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Step 2: Academic Profile">
    Define your academic commitments and subjects.
    
    **Required information:**
    - **Academic Level** - Choose from ESO, Bachillerato, Universidad, or FP
    - **Subjects** - Add all your current subjects with difficulty ratings (1-5)
    - **Weekly Class Schedule** - Set your school/university hours for Monday-Friday
    - **Transportation** - Indicate if you need travel time to school
    
    **Adding Subjects:**
    1. Enter the subject name (minimum 3 characters)
    2. Select difficulty level (1=Easy, 5=Very Hard)
    3. Click the + button or press Enter
    4. Repeat for all subjects
    
    <Tip>
    Be honest about difficulty ratings - the routine engine uses these to:
    - Allocate more study time to harder subjects
    - Calculate cognitive energy costs
    - Prioritize study sessions
    </Tip>
    
    **Class Schedule Setup:**
    - Configure your daily class hours using the schedule rows
    - Each day can have a start and end time
    - Leave days blank if you don't have classes
    - The system automatically blocks these hours in your routine
  </Step>

  <Step title="Step 3: Activities & Sports">
    Set up your athletic and extracurricular commitments.
    
    **Main Activity (Required):**
    - Enter your primary sport or physical activity
    - Select intensity level: Baja, Moderada, Alta, or Muy Alta
    - Define your weekly training schedule (all 7 days available)
    - Indicate if you need transportation to training
    
    **Secondary Activity (Optional):**
    - Add a second activity like languages, music, or other hobbies
    - Configure its weekly schedule separately
    - This helps the system account for all your commitments
    
    <Warning>
    The routine engine calculates physical load based on your activity intensity. High-intensity activities will reduce the amount of cognitive work scheduled on training days.
    </Warning>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Step 4: Daily Habits & Wellness">
    Fine-tune your daily routine with sleep, meals, and hydration goals.
    
    **Sleep Schedule:**
    - **Wake Time** - When you typically wake up (default: 07:30)
    - **Bed Time** - When you go to sleep (default: 23:00)
    - These define the boundaries for your daily routine generation
    
    **Meal Schedule:**
    - **Breakfast** - Default: 07:00
    - **Lunch** - Default: 15:00
    - **Dinner** - Default: 22:00
    - The system uses these as visual anchors in your timeline
    
    **Hydration Goal:**
    - Set your daily water intake target (1000-4000ml)
    - Default: 2000ml
    - Track your progress throughout the day
    
    <Accordion title="Can I change these settings later?">
      Yes! All onboarding settings can be updated in your profile settings at any time. Your routine will automatically regenerate based on the new information.
    </Accordion>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What Happens After Onboarding?

Once you complete the wizard (by clicking "Finish" on Step 4):

1. **Profile Creation** - Your complete user profile is validated and saved
2. **First Routine Generation** - The routine engine immediately generates your first optimized daily schedule
3. **Load Calculation** - Total cognitive and physical load is computed
4. **Backend Sync** - Your profile is saved to the cloud (if signed in)
5. **Dashboard Redirect** - You're taken to your personalized dashboard

<Note>
The routine engine uses a sophisticated solver algorithm that:
- Creates fixed blocks for school, training, and events
- Identifies free time slots
- Prioritizes tasks by difficulty and importance  
- Allocates study time using your focus preferences (Pomodoro by default)
- Respects the maximum daily energy load (5500 units)
</Note>

## Tips for Optimal Setup

<Accordion title="Be realistic with your schedule">
  Don't overcommit! The system will try to fill all available time. Leave some breathing room for spontaneous activities and rest.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Use accurate difficulty ratings">
  Your subject difficulty ratings directly impact study time allocation. A difficulty 5 subject gets 100 minutes of study time (5 × 20min multiplier) while a difficulty 2 subject gets only 40 minutes.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Account for transportation">
  If you commute to school or training, check the transportation boxes. The system will add buffer time to your schedule.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Adjust sleep schedule seasonally">
  Your sleep schedule might change during different seasons or academic periods. Update it in settings when needed.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Start with default focus strategy">
  The system uses Pomodoro (25min focus / 5min break) by default. Try it for a week before customizing in settings.
</Accordion>

## Skipping Onboarding

<Warning>
While you can technically skip onboarding, it's **strongly discouraged**. The routine engine requires your profile data to generate meaningful schedules. Without onboarding:
- No routines will be generated
- Dashboard widgets will show empty states
- You'll need to complete it eventually to use core features
</Warning>

## Validation Rules

The wizard enforces these validation rules:

| Field | Rule |
|-------|------|
| Name | Minimum 3 characters, letters and spaces only |
| Age | Must be 8 or older |
| Academic Level | Required selection |
| Subjects | At least 1 subject required |
| Subject Name | Minimum 3 characters, alphanumeric |
| Main Activity | Required, minimum 3 characters |
| Activity Level | Required selection |
